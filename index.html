The 2020 presidential election has been filled with mis-information, barriers to voting, and threats of undermining the democratic process of voting. We are grateful for Election Officials at county and state levels who have committed to protect our democracy and run the elections as smoothly as possible. We want the same.

We are asking that County Election Officials publicly declare your pledge to count all eligible votes and make the voting process as transparent and fair as possible given the current voting laws. We want people to know that they should feel encouraged to vote and that you will play your role in defending the voting rights they have. Please view and sign the pledge below.

We are also asking that members of the public pledge to be Election Defenders by publicly declaring support to Election Officials to stop any possible harassment from people wanting to interfere in the collection and counting of votes. We are concerned about scenarios like the “Brooks Brothers Riot” in 2000 where Miami-Dade recount workers were intimidated to stop counting votes. We don’t want to see that happen and ask that members of the public pledge to stand up against this type of harassment and interference. Election Defenders also pledge to make sure that Election Officials are doing as much as possible to make the process go smoothly and if not, push them to correct their course. Please read and sign the pledge below.
